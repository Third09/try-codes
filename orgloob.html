Student Development and Leadership Organization (SDLO)

THE STUDENT DEVELOPMENT AND LEADERSHIP ORGANIZATION (SDLO) IS A DYNAMIC GROUP COMMITTED TO FOSTERING LEADERSHIP SKILLS, PERSONAL GROWTH, AND COMMUNITY INVOLVEMENT AMONG STUDENTS. SDLO SERVES AS A PLATFORM FOR STUDENTS TO ENGAGE IN MEANINGFUL ACTIVITIES, DEVELOP CRITICAL THINKING, AND PROMOTE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY WITHIN THE SCHOOL AND THE LOCAL COMMUNITY.
